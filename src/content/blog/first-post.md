I don't even keep count of how many times I've torn my personal website down and built it back up anymore.

This time, it's more of a digital rebirth for me. I have gradually deleted my social media accounts over the past few years and want my digital presence to serve more of a purpose. One thing people I've worked with in real life have agreed on is that I'm good at expressing my thoughts in writing form so I guess I will play to that strength. 

At my company, I'd rather write documentation or a blog post over speaking to a large group of people any day so this tracks.

In the past, I would've wanted to write for younger people in computer science programs or already working on the web. Maybe some career advice? However, my career was built at a very different time. It's just so different. 

What do I have to offer, in that case? Well, my never-ending journey of learning never seems to subside so for now, that's what I want to put out there: what I'm learning about.

And yes, I actually talk like this.
