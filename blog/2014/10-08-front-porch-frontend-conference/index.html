After months of not going to any kind of developer gathering, I attended the FrontPorch conference which is a frontend web developer conference at the Granada Theater. Among topics covered were some that fairly interested me including SMACSS (metholodgy), best practices for SVG, deploying with Capistrano, and Node.

Check it out, www.frontporch.io.
